# Advisory Services & Rates

**One line:** Value-based advisory (fractional-CMO posture) priced on proprietary
systems and results — never on hours.

## Advisory ladder (sold via Gumroad → moving to own site)
| Tier | Price | Format |
|---|---|---|
| **Apoyo Express** | **$300** | 60 min |
| **Strategy Sprint** | **$550** | 90 min + deliverables |
| **VIP · 3 Actos** | **$1,200** | three 90-min sessions |

- **Venezuela discount:** 20% country discount → **$400** Strategy Sprint; two-part
  payment and credit card accepted.
- Positioning: **fractional CMO / strategic partner** — not executor or traditional agency.
- Delivery method = the [[Coaching Framework]]; targets both Client A (early-stage) and
  Client B (established, scaling).

## Brand-partnership / UGC rate rules
- **Never quote rates verbally on calls.**
- **Production costs are fixed** regardless of distribution — reject CPM/performance
  models as a default.
- **Usage rate card** (on top of base content fee):
  | Usage window | % of content fee |
  |---|---|
  | 30 days | 30% |
  | 60 days | 40% |
  | 90 days | 50% |
  | Perpetual | 100% |
- Paid usage, whitelisting, exclusivity, and revisions are **quoted separately**.
- **Signed contract + cleared deposit before production begins.**
- **Net-new scope = paid addition**, unless she explicitly decides otherwise *before* the
  partner responds.
- Verify brand contacts via **official email domains** before advancing.
- Banking details go **separately** from contracts ("provided upon request").
- California governing law is standard on brand-side contracts — leverage, not a dealbreaker on small deals.

## Client segmentation
- **Client A** — early-stage creator/small business, not yet monetizing → low-ticket digital products.
- **Client B** — established, scaling, running campaigns without creative-strategy structure → advisory/workshops/systems. *(Current launch focus.)*
