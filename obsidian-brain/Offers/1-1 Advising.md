# Asesorías 1:1 (1:1 Advising)

**One line:** One-on-one strategy advising / "Strategy Sprint" — each client gets
their own Notion space built from a shared template.

## How it's structured
- Every **Asesoría 1:1** is its own page (a copy of the master template) inside the
  Marketing-Strategist space.
- The engagement runs on the **[[Coaching Framework]]** (5-block self-discovery) plus a
  standard set of deliverables per client:
  - **Estrategia (1 objetivo)** — focus the whole sprint on ONE goal (ventas / comunidad / …)
  - **Market Research** — 3 referentes + 3 competidores, 1 actionable insight each
  - **Idea bank** — 1 idea = 1 video
  - **Action Plan (14 días)** — priorities for the next two weeks on one page
  - **Intake form** · **Modelo "Monetiza" de tu oferta** · **Links de entregables** (session recording, etc.)
- Example benchmark used with clients: engagement rate **> 3%** (views-to-likes).

## Offer types seen
- **Asesoría 1:1** (full)
- **Strategy Sprint (Asesoría 1:1)**
- **Asesoría Prompting** (AI/prompting-focused)

> [!note] Privacy
> Individual client pages (names, personal answers, recordings) live in Notion and are
> **intentionally excluded** from this brain. Only the reusable method is captured here.
