# Creatividad² — Course & Funnel

**One line:** Her flagship course *"Crea Contenido Sin Complicarte"* — teach any
creative woman to go from idea to posted video without overcomplicating it.

## The offer
- **Course:** *Crea Contenido Sin Complicarte*
- **Price ladder:** $350 full · $250 (48h launch) · $200 (pre-access)
- **Structure:** 4 modules mirroring the [[Content Creation Method]]:
  1. Pre-producción (idea → guion) — hooks, script structures, AI, *banco de ideas + 30 hooks*
  2. Producción (grabar con lo que tienes) — light, angles, audio hacks, solo setups
  3. Edición (de básico a wow) — retention cuts, subtitles, transitions, presets
  4. Posting (llegar a más) — SEO captions, hashtags, timing, metrics, soft selling
- **Bonuses:** monthly live Q&A (3 mo), private WhatsApp/Telegram group, extra templates
  (scripts, storytelling ideas, editable annual calendar).

## The funnel — free diagnostic → course
- Lead magnet: the **[[Creator Maturity Model]]** quiz — *"¿Por qué tu contenido no
  convierte?"* — 12 questions, no signup, personalized **5-minute plan** by email.
- Flow: landing → router (creator vs business) → 4 Qs → break → 4 Qs → break → 4 Qs →
  result (bottleneck bucket + social proof %) → email capture → thank-you + **referral
  loop** (both people get priority + special price on Creatividad², referrer gets the
  *Agenda Crea tu 2026* free).
- Built/automated with **n8n**; brand colors + Poppins per [[Brand Guide]].
