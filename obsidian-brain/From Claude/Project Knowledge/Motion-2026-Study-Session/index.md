## Curriculum overview

The bootcamp runs 8 weeks; this corpus covers weeks 1‚Äì7 (week 8 not yet recorded). The arc is **Foundations ‚Üí Sprints**:

- **Foundations (Wks 1‚Äì5)** ‚Äî Build the creative strategist's core operating model: hooks (1) ‚Üí research (2) ‚Üí prioritize/diagnose (3) ‚Üí produce ads with AI (4) ‚Üí analyze (5).
- **Sprints (Wks 6‚Äì8)** ‚Äî Apply foundations in compressed cycles: iterate on first ads (6) ‚Üí adapt winners into new formats (7) ‚Üí big swing capstone (8, not yet recorded).
- **Tuesday classes** = single-instructor or guest-panel deep dive with slides.
- **Thursday group coaching** = panel of Motion strategists + invited coaches doing live student work review and tactic walkthroughs. Coaching sessions contain dense **student Q&A**, **ads critiqued live**, and **live worked examples** (often Claude/Motion screenshares).

A throughline across nearly every session is **Meta's Andromeda algorithm**: it has shifted creative strategy from minor variant testing to meaningful pivots, made hook diversity primary, and weakened classical A/B testing. Dates referenced are 2026.
