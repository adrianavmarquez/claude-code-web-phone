### Slide #21 — "Grocery List" Mindset
- **Timestamps**: 23:39
- **Layout**: bullet list
- **Title / header text**: "Grocery List" Mindset
- **Body content**: 
    - Whenever you are doing ideation, go into it after doing an analysis.
    - This way you KNOW what you need, e.g. "a static image w/ a headline and 3 benefits", or "a video hook that agitates the problem a lot".
    - Otherwise you'll be wandering swipe file platforms and inspo boards aimlessly, and waste a lot of time.
    - Or, for many, "ideation" = "let's see my favorite guru posted on twitter/linkedin" (I'm guilty of this myself at times)
    - Ideation should take less than 10 min if you do a good analysis and have this "grocery list" mindset.
    - Like going to the supermarket with a grocery list vs. without one - just way more efficient
