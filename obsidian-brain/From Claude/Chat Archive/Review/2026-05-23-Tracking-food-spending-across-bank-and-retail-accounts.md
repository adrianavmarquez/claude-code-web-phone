# Tracking food spending across bank and retail accounts

Here's what you can do instead, and I can help with the heavy lifting once you have the files:

**Option 1: Export and upload**
- Chase: Download your transaction history as a CSV from chase.com (Accounts > Download Activity)
- Amazon: Download your order history report from Amazon's "Order History Reports" page (account settings)
- Upload both files here and I'll cross-reference them, categorize everything (food, other items, fees), and give you a clean breakdown

**Option 2: Use a dedicated tool**
Apps like **Copilot**, **Monarch Money**, or **YNAB** connect directly to Chase and can auto-categorize. They're built for exactly this.

If you go the CSV route, I can build you a full spending breakdown with categories, totals, and even a visual chart. Want to try that?
