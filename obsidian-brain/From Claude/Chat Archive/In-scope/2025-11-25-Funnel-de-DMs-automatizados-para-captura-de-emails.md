# Funnel de DMs automatizados para captura de emails

> **Conversation Overview**

The user is working on improving their automated DM funnel in Manychat for lead generation and email collection. They wanted to create a new funnel specifically triggered when people comment "VOZ" on a video they posted, which would then send them a workbook titled "Cómo Crear Contenido Original sin Perder Tu Esencia" (How to Create Original Content Without Losing Your Essence). The user's video showed them starting to record without knowing what to say, then letting the content flow naturally - which directly connected to the workbook's theme about finding one's authentic voice.

The user provided an existing funnel example as reference (triggered by "PRO") and outlined three key objectives: building an email database for remarketing, maintaining natural conversational flow that matches their writing style, and motivating people to willingly share their email addresses without resistance. Claude initially attempted to access the workbook link but couldn't retrieve the content directly. After seeing the video content, Claude created a four-message DM sequence that connected the video moment (being stuck but continuing to record) with the workbook's value proposition about finding one's authentic voice without forcing it.

The user made an important correction when Claude used "la neta" (Mexican slang), clarifying they don't use that phrase. The user then adapted Claude's first message, changing "HEY ✨" to "HELLO HELLO ✨" and restructuring the opening to more directly connect the video's blank moment with the workbook's benefit in a single, flowing sentence. This demonstrated the user's preference for their own authentic voice and natural phrasing in their marketing communications.
