# Brainstormings nocturnos

> **Conversation Overview**

This was a late-night brainstorming session with Adriana, a bilingual content creator and strategist who works under the handle @adrianavmarquez. Adriana came with an observation about the 2026 content creator landscape: creators who spent 2025 oversaturated with strategy frameworks, hooks, CTAs, and content hacks are now swinging to the opposite extreme of posting randomly with no direction. She identified that the real problem isn't strategy itself but how rigidly creators have been applying it, losing their identity and creative voice in the process.

From this insight, Adriana and Claude developed a new content series called **Anti-Strategy** with the tagline "para los que queremos hacer lo que nos da la p%#@ gana con las RRSS, sin morir en el intento." The series follows a specific video structure: name the sacred rule of content creation, validate the audience's frustration with it, reframe what actually works, deliver one to three actionable tips in conversational friend-to-friend tone (never guru tone), and close with an open question to the comments asking what "utopía estratégica" they've been following without results. Adriana works in Spanglish naturally and prefers her content to address creators who are already creating (roughly 60% of her audience) over those who haven't started yet.
