# Script de video corto para creadores con quiz diagnóstico

> **Conversation Overview**

This conversation centered on developing a short-form video script for a Spanish-language content creator who is both the subject and the brand. The creator, working in the content strategy and creator education space, needed a video that would function organically but be built with paid media logic in mind, as she may boost it later. The core goal was to create a hook-to-CTA pipeline that mirrors creator pain, delivers a genuinely shareable golden nugget, establishes credibility, and funnels viewers toward a free interactive quiz she built to diagnose content weaknesses across six stages of a content pipeline.

The conversation evolved iteratively through multiple rounds of revision. Claude drafted an initial structure (hook, trust badge, chain metaphor, CTA), and the creator progressively rewrote and sharpened it in her own voice, introducing key elements: the VOC framework (Ver, Oír, Comprobar) as her proprietary hook-quality test, specific comedic examples in Caribbean Spanish ("me fue peor que a un sordo en clases de dictado"), a before/after credibility moment using real account growth metrics overlaid on screen, and the ManyChat keyword "NUCA" tied to the self-diagnosis metaphor ("verte la nuca"). Claude flagged structural issues throughout—including a number conflict between the VOC's "3" and the quiz's bucket count—and the creator confirmed the quiz diagnoses six stages, resolving the conflict. A key visual decision was made to show six blurred buckets on screen with only the hook bucket legible, creating a visual open loop that makes the quiz feel like the only way to "unblur" the viewer's specific weakness.

The creator's voice preferences are explicit and non-negotiable for organic content: profanity, colloquialisms ("alv," "mi amorchhh," "valiendo verga"), Caribbean Spanish expressions, and direct address are core to her brand identity and were preserved throughout. She flagged that paid-safe language softening was not needed since this is her own channel with no brand partners involved. Claude's role shifted across the conversation from script generator to strategic editor and creative director, flagging logic gaps, payoff misalignments, and visual-audio coordination issues (e.g., stats appearing on the word "ahora," not before; the blur needing two full seconds on screen to register the open loop). The creator corrected one misunderstanding mid-conversation—she had initially said "3 etapas" then confirmed she meant 6—and Claude adjusted accordingly. The final approved script runs approximately 64 seconds across six beats: hook, VOC reveal, credibility moment, VOC nugget with checklist overlay, blurred-buckets bridge with the "nuca" metaphor, and CTA with the NUCA keyword trigger for ManyChat.
