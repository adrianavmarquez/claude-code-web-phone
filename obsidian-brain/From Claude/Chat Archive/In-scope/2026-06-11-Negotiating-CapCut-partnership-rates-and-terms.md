# Negotiating CapCut partnership rates and terms

> **Conversation Overview**

Adriana Márquez, a content creator, sought help navigating a brand deal negotiation with CapCut. The conversation involved analyzing incoming offers, drafting counter-proposals, and evaluating the legitimacy of the partnership contact. The negotiation centered on a standalone Instagram Reel deliverable, with Adriana's standard flat rate as the baseline. The brand contact is named Jackey Sinon, and all communication was handled over email.

The core negotiation tension was CapCut's push toward a performance-based CPM or milestone model versus Adriana's principle that production costs are fixed regardless of distribution outcomes. Claude helped her analyze and counter their proposed base-plus-bonus structure, with emphasis on three strategic points: anchoring the guaranteed base high enough to cover production, ensuring her flat rate is reached at an early and achievable milestone rather than treated as the ceiling, and structuring the upside so the maximum payout meaningfully exceeds her standard rate to justify taking on performance risk. Claude also advised shifting the measurement window from 7 days to 14 days as the negotiating anchor, rather than 30 days, on the grounds that 14 is defensible and avoids unnecessary friction.

When CapCut retracted their prior proposal and pivoted to four alternative cooperation models involving brand-edited content or voiceover-only appearances, Adriana flagged concern about the legitimacy of the contact. Claude assessed the situation as showing soft red flags worth monitoring, including the retracted concrete proposal, the pivot to lower-trust formats, and the burden-shifting tactic of asking her to quote across multiple models, while noting that no hard financial scam indicators had appeared. Claude provided a decisive verification step (checking the sender's email domain against official CapCut or ByteDance domains) and drafted a clean professional close declining the alternative models, restating her standard terms, and leaving a narrow door open for future contact on her terms. Adriana's stated working preferences include flat-fee contracts only, signed agreements through secure e-signature platforms, and a partial deposit before any production begins, with herself always positioned as creator of record.
