# Plataforma del sitio de presupuestos de diseño

> **Conversation Overview**

The person was investigating the technology stack behind a specific lead capture funnel URL and exploring alternatives for building similar quiz-style funnels. Claude identified the site as built on GoHighLevel (GHL), noting the telltale signs: the `.guaranteedquotes.biz` subdomain pattern and CDN assets served from `leadconnectorhq.com` and `filesafe.space`. The funnel style was described as a multi-step quiz format that feels app-like.

The conversation then shifted to evaluating alternative platforms for building this type of step-by-step question/answer funnel. Claude organized options by category: Typeform, Tally.so, and Jotform for quiz-style flows; Interact, ScoreApp, and Outgrow for dedicated quiz funnels; ClickFunnels and Leadpages for full funnel builders; and Framer or Webflow for fully custom, brand-controlled builds. Claude noted GHL has a low design ceiling and recommended Typeform or a custom Framer/Webflow build for higher brand quality.

The person then clarified they already have their own CRM and want to use a landing page primarily for lead data collection and pixel-based retargeting. Claude outlined a stack: a custom landing in Framer or Webflow for full pixel control, Google Tag Manager to manage Meta and TikTok pixels with granular custom events per funnel step (e.g., `Lead_Step1`, `FormCompleted`), and Make or Zapier to route form submissions to their CRM via API. The conversation ended with Claude asking which CRM the person uses to provide more specific integration guidance.
