> **Conversation Overview**

The person is working on a video script for social media content and sought Claude's help rewriting it with a specific tone shift. The collaboration was highly iterative. The person provided their original script in a detailed production table format with columns for on-screen text, dialogue/voiceover, visuals, audio, and effects. Throughout the session, the person made direct line-level edits, rejecting or refining specific phrases in real time. Estás construyendo tu Pinterest mental."

The person communicates in Spanish, writes in a casual and direct register, and has a strong instinct for when language sounds too produced or over-explained.
