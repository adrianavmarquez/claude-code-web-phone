# Politely declining unpaid brand collaborations

> **Conversation Overview**

Adriana is a content creator who handles brand collaboration inquiries and needed help drafting polite decline messages for two separate outreach pitches. Her clear stated policy is that she only accepts paid collaborations and does not take unpaid arrangements, including product credit or store credit exchanges.

The first inquiry was from Joy at Yesglasses, offering store credit in exchange for a dedicated try-on reel with usage rights for Meta branded content ads. Claude drafted two response options: one that firmly declined while opening the door to a paid partnership (noting that the Meta ad usage component is typically a paid line item), and one that was a clean, final decline. Claude also flagged that the brand's mention of boosting content as paid media ads is a meaningful distinction worth addressing if Adriana wanted to counter-propose.

The second inquiry was from Poornima at Lingopanda, a language learning app, who had not yet disclosed compensation terms and was simply asking if Adriana was open to hearing more. Adriana indicated she was not interested at this time. Claude offered two response options—a warm pass that left the door open for future opportunities, and a shorter, cleaner decline—and noted that since the offer terms were still unknown, Adriana could also ask for more details before deciding if she preferred that approach.
