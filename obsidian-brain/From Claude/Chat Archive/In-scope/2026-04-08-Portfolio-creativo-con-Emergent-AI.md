# Portfolio creativo con Emergent AI

> **Conversation Overview**

Adriana (@adrianavmarquez) is a full-time Venezuelan content creator working on a paid brand collaboration with Emergent AI, a platform that converts ideas into production-ready websites and apps using AI. The entire conversation centered on developing a sponsored reel script and supporting deliverables for this collab. Adriana's creative concept involves building her actual portfolio on Emergent and documenting the process, framing the narrative around how her portfolio never matched her creative level until she discovered the platform. The brand contact is Kritika Bhatia (kritika@emergent.sh), Brand Manager at Emergent, who communicates in English.

The session involved iterative script development, moving from a rough creative brief to a finalized beat-by-beat script. Key decisions made: the hook settled on a confessional tone ("Mi portafolio daba pena ajena. Honestamente no sé cómo he cobrado lo que he cobrado con eso."), the format is a mix of on-camera talking head and portfolio demo scroll, and the closing line "Ahora mi creatividad, mi talento y mi portfolio están al mismo nivel" was Adriana's own addition and identified as the strongest line in the script. The product description line was refined collaboratively to: "Hasta que conocí Emergent AI. Una plataforma donde conviertes tus ideas en websites y apps reales, listas para usar. Tú te enfocas en pensar exactamente lo que quieres, lo describes bien. Y ya. Lo construyó." The script has not yet been approved — Adriana is awaiting brand feedback before building the portfolio and shooting.

Two email drafts were composed: one submitting the initial script to Kritika with context and a full English transcript, and a second responding to Kritika's revision requests. Kritika asked for two changes: a stronger, more shocking hook that mentions Emergent earlier, and the addition of content creation tips for beginner creators woven into the script. Adriana responded by generating eight hook options in both Spanish and English, with Emergent named in the first few seconds of each. Adriana also corrected option 8 to reflect her actual situation: "A full-time content creator with an embarrassing portfolio. Then I found Emergent. One of these things had to change." The next step is awaiting Kritika's hook selection before updating the script with the beginner creator tips beat and regenerating the document.

A branded .docx script document was generated using a Node.js skill with the docx library. The document structure is fixed: header with brand colors, beat-by-beat table (3 columns: Visual / Script / Notas de dirección), overlays approval table (2 columns: Tipo / Contenido), production notes, and visual references. Brand aesthetics are non-negotiable: Poppins font, red #D72323 for section titles, blue #3846C4 for table headers and stars, yellow #FFBA35 for header text and secondary stars, white rows with black text for data rows, alternating white/gray rows in the beats table. A key aesthetic update was made mid-session: both tables (beats and overlays) were changed to have blue headers with yellow text, replacing the previous white-header-red-text and dark-background-white-text styles respectively. Adriana attempted to update the skill files directly but could not due to read-only permissions, so the updated SKILL.md and generate.js were provided as downloadable files for manual replacement in the skill directory at `/mnt/skills/user/script-skill/`.
