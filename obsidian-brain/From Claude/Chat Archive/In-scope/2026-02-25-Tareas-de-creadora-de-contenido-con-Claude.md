## 🤖 Claude · 2026-02-25 16:00
Great question! For a content creator, here's my take:

**Go with the 32GB RAM + 512GB storage option.**

RAM is the most important factor for your workflow. Video editing (especially 4K), running editing software like Final Cut Pro or Premiere, having multiple apps open simultaneously — all of this benefits massively from 32GB vs 16GB. With 16GB you might start hitting limitations sooner than you'd expect, especially as your projects grow.

Storage is easier to solve externally. A fast external SSD (like Samsung T7 or similar) gives you plenty of affordable extra space for project files and archives, and it's a fraction of the cost of upgrading RAM — which you *can't* add later on a Mac Mini.

So the logic is: **max out what you can't change (RAM), and expand what you can (storage).**

That said, if budget is a real concern and you're mainly editing short-form content (Reels, TikToks, YouTube vlogs rather than heavy productions), the 16GB + 1TB could work fine for now. But for long-term investment, 32GB is the smarter pick for a creator at your level.
