"Video skits" are short, comedic video segments - basically brief scripted scenes or performances designed to be funny or entertaining. However, I think you might be asking about "skids" (not "skids" as a misspelling of "skits"). In internet slang, particularly in gaming and hacking communities, a "skid" or "script kiddie" refers to someone who uses pre-made tools, scripts, or hacks created by others without really understanding how they work. It's generally used as a derogatory term for someone who pretends to have technical skills they don't actually possess.
