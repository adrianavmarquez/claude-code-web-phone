# Content Pillars

**One line:** Four pillars — Beauty, Fashion, Conscious Lifestyle, Art & Creative
Expression — all in service of confidence and self-expression.

## The four pillars
1. **Belleza (Beauty)** — perfume as personal expression and *the best accessory*.
   Products shown casually in stories and inside video-editing tutorials, where the
   content orbits *subtly* around a product she wants to highlight.
2. **Moda (Fashion)** — creative outfits; the series *"¿Qué me pongo si…?"*; practical
   tips; inspiring carousels.
3. **Lifestyle consciente** — wellness, organization, mental health, a realistic +
   motivating routine.
4. **Arte & expresión creativa** — photography, audiovisual storytelling, video as a
   vehicle for identity and confidence.

## Formats she publishes in
- **Short video (15–30s):** quick tips, strong hooks, visual humor.
- **Voiceover storytelling:** personal experiences, reflections, motivation.
- **Carousels:** tips, monthly favorites, fashion/beauty gear.
- **Long YouTube:** tutorials, Q&A, creative education, day-in-the-life vlogs.

## Recurring series
- **Hay Talento** — 90 tips series (talent-spotlight energy).
- **Q&A TikTok**
- **90 días, 90 tips**
- **¿Qué me pongo si…?** (fashion scenarios)
- **Cosas que no me importan** (Sunday, playful)
