# Brand Guide

**One line:** Natural, vibrant, fun, with an editorial edge — her face and
expression are the anchor of every thumbnail.

## Visual identity
- **Aesthetic:** natural, vibrant, playful, with an editorial touch.
- **Positioning to grow into:** *directora creativa* (creative director) with digital
  presence — documenting a professional process and showing **criterion**, not just tips.
  *"Los tips son universales. El criterio es propietario."*

## Colors (from Creatividad² / Quiz brand system)
- **Red** `#D72323` — primary accent / CTA / the "bottleneck" signal
- **Yellow** `#FFBA35` — secondary / "areas to improve"
- **Blue** `#3846C4` — brand blue
- Lavender — calm/"healthy" states
- **Typeface:** Poppins (Bold / Black for heroes)
- Brand marks: the small monogram, a red star sticker, doodles (little stars, pluses, pixel art)

## Thumbnails
- Minimalist but eye-catching; **short text in CAPS (max 5–7 words)**; high contrast.
- Always leave room for her face — **her expression is key.**
- Reels/TikTok: expressive/emotive frames, on-screen text = summarized hook, one clear idea.
- YouTube podcast style: big expressive face, 3–5 words + ep###; LUT **FilmKid**.
- YouTube vlog style: she picks the cover frame, ≤5-word phrase; LUT **Everyday 33 or 65**.

## Platforms
TikTok · Instagram · Pinterest · YouTube
