# Positioning & Manifesto

**One line:** A Creative Marketing Strategist who rejects generic creator tips —
*"te sacudo, no te consiento"* — and creates from criterion, voice, and process.

## Who she is now (current positioning)
- **Creative Marketing Strategist**, content creator, and personal brand consultant, Charlotte NC.
- Operates **primarily in Spanish** for the US Latino market + Mexico, Spain, Venezuela, Colombia.
- Audience **~50–65K** followers, substantial monthly reach; growing an email list.
- Distinguished explicitly from generic creator advice — this is a **non-negotiable filter**
  on every content and copy decision. *"Visual curator and creative direction reference."*

## Manifesto & ethos
- **Manifesto:** *"My enemy is whoever hoards knowledge and doesn't share it with the masses."*
- **Coaching ethos:** *"Te sacudo, no te consiento."* — direct, experience-grounded
  challenge, never coddling or performance.
- **Anti-Strategy** positioning: rejects rigid frameworks in favor of **voice, process,
  and human irreplaceability**. *"Los tips son universales; el criterio es propietario."*

## The two content pillars that work together
1. **Opinion / Philosophy** → drives **shares + community**
2. **Technical Frameworks** → drives **saves + reach**

## Signature content series
- **Anti-Strategy**
- **Brand Dissect**
- **"Yo traigo el contenido, tú montas el café"** (the 90-tips format)
- **"Hay talento, lo que falta es apoyo"**

## What wins in 2026 (her thesis)
- *"3 guionados tips" are dead* (ChatGPT gives those); the infallible-expert loses traction.
- What wins: **document, don't create** (process + vulnerability), serialized content,
  strong industry opinions, visual essays.
- Creator as **protagonist of their own story**, not instructor. Teaching comes *after*
  messaging clarity, not before.
