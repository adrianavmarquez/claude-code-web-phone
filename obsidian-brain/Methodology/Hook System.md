# Hook System

**One line:** Every piece opens with a hook in the first 3 seconds, built across
three modes — visual, textual, audio — and rotated by hook *type*.

## The 3 modes (fire all three)
- **Visual:** the frame filters the audience. Real work setup (monitor, notebook)
  attracts people who *work*, not people who decorate offices.
- **Textual:** on-screen hook with a **specific** claim, not a generic one.
  ❌ *"3 páginas para música"* → ✅ *"Si todavía usas Epidemic Sound en 2026 estás
  sonando como tu competencia."*
- **Audio:** tone of a **creative director** giving direction — declarative,
  certain, less "creator giving tips."

## Hook types (the rotation)
- **Afirmación audaz** — bold claim, uses natural controversy of the category.
- **Curiosidad / open loop** — best when the topic is technical, not confrontational.
- **Contraste** — separate two things that look alike ("saving references ≠ having criterion").
- **Confesión** — confessional angle ("I quit Pinterest 2 years ago and my work got better").
- **Identificación** — call out exactly who it's for.

## Beat structure of a talking-head reel (≤60s)
```
0–3s    HOOK · claim with criterion + audience filter
3–8s    CONTEXTO · when she really uses this (real process)
8–20s   POINT 1 · name + why + what kind of project
20–32s  POINT 2 · same structure, contrast with #1
32–45s  POINT 3 · the one nobody names — authority by exclusivity
45–55s  "LO QUE NO USO Y POR QUÉ" · the differentiating beat
55–60s  CTA · "sigue pa más recursos con criterio" (not "guarda esto")
```

## Variable beats (keep a series from feeling like a template)
- **"Lo que no uso y por qué"** — activate when the category has an obvious villain.
- **"Combinación real"** — when two resources are used together in one real project (shows workflow).
- **"El que está en beta / nadie nombra"** — early-access positioning as the strong close.

## The principle
*"No estás listando. Estás documentando un proceso profesional, mostrando criterio de
descarte."* Tips are universal; **criterion is proprietary.**
