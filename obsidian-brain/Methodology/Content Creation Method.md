# Content Creation Method

**One line:** *"Crea contenido sin complicarte"* — a 4-stage pipeline from idea to
posted video, designed to unblock, not to impress.

This is the backbone taught in **[[Creatividad2 Course]]** and applied to her own content.

## The pipeline
### 1. Pre-producción — idea → guion
- Find ideas without going crazy: pull from a **banco de ideas**, not from thin air.
- **Hook frameworks** → questions, comparisons, controversy, identification (see [[Hook System]]).
- Script structures: mini-tutorial · storytelling · educational (*vender sin vender*).
- Use AI (ChatGPT) to accelerate ideas and scripts.
- Benchmark: write a first script in **15 minutes**.

### 2. Producción — record like a pro with what you have
- Natural light + a cheap ring light; flattering angles (phone + tripod).
- Cheap external mic or no-mic audio hacks.
- Record solo: tripod, timer, remote apps.
- Resolve **light and clean audio first**. End with a main take **+ cutaways**, not one take.

### 3. Edición — from basic to wow
- Fast cuts and rhythm for retention.
- **Auto-subtitles always** — they set the pace.
- Creative text (curves, behind-the-subject), easy transitions, music synced to hook the first second.
- Presets + a publishing checklist; adapt per format (Reels/Shorts/TikTok).

### 4. Posting — reach more
- **SEO-friendly captions** with a hook; strategic hashtags (3 layers, see [[Voice & Tone]]).
- Adapt each post **per platform** (don't copy-paste identical).
- **Soft selling** — sell without sounding like selling.
- Read metrics without overcomplicating: watch **saves, watch time, shares** at 48h.
- Repurpose one video across platforms.

## The through-line
Idea from a system → strong hook in 3s → clean capture → retention-first edit →
platform-adapted post → learn from the winners and **make the win repeatable** (turn it
into a manual). That last move — *"entender por qué y volverlo manual"* — is the top
score in the [[Creator Maturity Model]].
