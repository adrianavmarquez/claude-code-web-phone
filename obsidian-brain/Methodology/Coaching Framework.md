# Coaching Framework — 1:1 Strategic Self-Discovery

**One line:** The reusable method Adriana runs in her 1:1 sessions to pull a client's
**voice, story, and content pillars** out of "I just know about X."

> [!note] Methodology only
> This note captures the *framework* — the individual clients' private answers stay in
> Notion and are intentionally not copied into the brain.

## Session philosophy
- **Don't cage the client early.** Pillars are closed *at the end*, not the start.
- *"Tu voz vive en los ejemplos concretos, no en las definiciones."* → always re-ask
  to land a vague answer into a concrete example.
- What a client **rejects** defines their voice as much as what they love.

## The 5 blocks (≈75 min)
| # | Block | What it extracts | ~min |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Lo que ya sabes** | What they *truly* master and can teach | 20 |
| 2 | **Tu voz y tu tono** | How they talk when they're really themselves | 15 |
| 3 | **Tu historia** | Personal experiences that become content | 15 |
| 4 | **Reaccionar a referencias** | Which formats resonate → candidate pillars | 15 |
| 5 | **Cerrar juntas** | Final pillars + signature phrases | 10 |

## Signature exercises
- **"Lista de 20"** — 20 things you know about your topic, unfiltered. *Each one can be a reel.*
- **"Carta a [tú] de hace 2 años"** — everything you know now and didn't then is
  **gold content**, because someone is standing exactly where you were.
- **Nota de voz cruda** — record 2 min talking like it's your friend → mine the real
  voice, phrases, and tone from it.
- **Reacción a referencias** — per reel: *"¿esto suena a mí? ¿lo haría? ¿qué jamás haría?"*
- Prompt: *"La gente cree que [tema] es (…), pero en realidad es (…)."*

## The transformation it drives
From *"sé de X"* → *"enseño exactamente ESTO, con MI voz, desde MI experiencia."*

## Related session artifacts (in Notion, per client)
Estrategia (1 objetivo) · Market Research · Idea bank · Action Plan (14 días) ·
Intake form · Modelo "Monetiza" de tu oferta · Links de entregables.
