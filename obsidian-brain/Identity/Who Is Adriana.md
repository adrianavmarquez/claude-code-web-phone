# Who Is Adriana

**One line:** Adriana Márquez — Venezuelan **Creative Marketing Strategist**, 28, in
Charlotte, NC — who helps overwhelmed creative women (and Latino brands) turn scattered
ideas and untapped talent into content they actually publish.

## Mission
Empower creative women who feel **overwhelmed** — full of ideas and talent but stuck
on *how to start* or *how to get better*. Promote self-love through discipline,
wellness, free expression, and mental health — *"because without that, there's no
creativity that lasts, that's worth it, or that stays."*

## Values

- **Empower > Impress** — give women confidence and a voice
- Creativity cannot exist without self-love, discipline, wellness, and mental health
- Inclusion · creativity · simplicity · humor · immigrant resilience
