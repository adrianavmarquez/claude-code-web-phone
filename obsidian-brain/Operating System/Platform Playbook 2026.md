# Platform Playbook 2026

**One line:** How each platform actually distributes in 2026 — and the one metric that
matters on each.

## TikTok
- Followers are **gatekeepers** — test with them first.
- Target **completion rate ≥ 70%**.
- **Shares are worth ~5× likes.**
- Penalizes repetitive formats.
- Only place for the signature close: **"nos vemos manianaaa ✨"**.

## Instagram — *"Interest Media"*
- Distribution is by **interest, not relationships**.
- Metric priority: **#1 Saves** → Watch Time (%) → Share Rate → long comments.

## YouTube
- **Connected ecosystem:** Shorts + long-form = one unified signal.
- **CTR + AVD** still win.
- **Semantic SEO** replaced hashtags.

## Cross-platform rules
- **Adapt per platform** — never copy-paste identical (see [[Content Creation Method]]).
- Route CTAs to **owned properties** (newsletter, YouTube) over bare follow requests.
- Bilingual on organic social; **English** for professional/client-facing content.
- Register keyword triggers in ManyChat for lead flows (e.g. *NUCA* → diagnostic video,
  *MASARI* → rate calculator).
